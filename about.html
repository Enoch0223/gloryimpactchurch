About Glory Impact Church

Glory Impact Apostolic Global Ministry is a Nigerian-based Christian ministry founded by Pastor (Dr.) John Adebowale Adeyemi. The ministry is located in Lagos, Nigeria and is known as a sanctuary of grace, truth, and mercy. Glory Impact is an apostolic ministry geared towards a global harvesting of souls.

We believe in the unity of the Godhead and the distinctions between the members of the Trinity. They teach the need for conviction of sin, repentance, restitution, and confession for salvation.

The ministry is geared towards a global harvesting of souls and preparing people for the coming of the Lord Jesus Christ.

Our Vision

"To activate your ordained Glory, live your life in full and die empty by releasing your potential for the benefit of the universe and to reign with God eternally."

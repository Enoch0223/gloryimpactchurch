PASTOR MRS. RUTH ENIOLA ADEYEMI

Co-Pastor & Family Life Director of Glory Impact Apostolic Global Ministry

Pastor Mrs. Ruth Eniola Oluwabunmi Adeyemi is a born teacher with the passion to minister to families, most especially teaching and raising godly children for the glory of God. She is born in May 3rd, 1975.

She lived part of her life in Kano State with her parents, where she schooled and obtain BSc. in Political Science at Bayero University (BUK) in 1998. She got married to Pastor John Debo in December 23rd, 2000. Pastor Eniola is civil servant with the Lagos State government at the State Universal Basic Education Board (SUBEB). But she is involved with the work of the ministry with her husband as a co-pastor in the ministry.

She operates in the office of a prophetess and evangelist and her ministrations have brought a lot of blessing to the church and the kingdom of God.

She loves singing and needle works. She resides in Lagos, Nigeria.

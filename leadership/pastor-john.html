PASTOR JOHN ADEBOWALE ADEYEMI

Senior Pastor & Founder of Glory Impact Apostolic Global Ministry

Pastor Dr. John Debo Adeyemi is an Apostolic, Prophetic Pastor and teacher with divine mandate to liberate the world from the grip of sin and hell and to release them into the fulfilment of their life and destiny.

He has been in the ministry for about three decades now; he is the president of Rhema Global Impact and the senior Pastor of Glory Impact Apostolic Global Ministry. A prolific writer, renowned author and researcher. He speaks in conferences, seminars, workshops and other events with great life-changing unction. He currently resides in Lagos with his lovely wife and glorious children.

When confused Pastor John Debo will always seek and pray for the wisdom of God for direction. The mere taste of true godliness that he received set him on fire with such a desire to progress that he pursue the rest of his studies with coolness.
